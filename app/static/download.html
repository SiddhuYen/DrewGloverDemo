2Install Ollama

Ollama runs a small language model on your own machine. Artemis uses it to judge whether two people in the network are actually connected from the wording on a page, on top of the deterministic rules that catch clear-cut cases like a team roster or a podcast credit.

macOS
Download Ollama
Windows
Download Ollama
Why this step exists: structural sources (rosters, filings, episode credits) are certain, so Artemis trusts them outright. Everything else — a sentence that merely implies a relationship — is fuzzy, and that's what the local model on Ollama is for instead of guessing with fixed rules.

3Pull the model Artemis uses

Once Ollama is installed, open a terminal and run:

ollama pull llama3.2

Placeholder model — a teammate will swap this for whichever model Artemis ships with once that's finalized.

4Launch Artemis

Open the app you downloaded in step 1 and sign in. Launch instructions will be filled in here once the installer exists.
