02

Install Ollama

Ollama runs a small language model on your own machine. Artemis uses it to judge whether two people in the network are actually connected from the wording on a page, on top of the deterministic rules that catch clear-cut cases like a team roster or a podcast credit.

Why this step exists

Structural sources, like rosters, filings, and episode credits, are certain, so Artemis trusts them outright. Everything else, a sentence that merely implies a relationship, is fuzzy. That is what the local model on Ollama is for, instead of guessing with fixed rules.

03

Pull the model Artemis uses

Once Ollama is installed, open a terminal and run:

ollama pull llama3.2
04

Launch Artemis

Open the app you downloaded in step 1. Your network is already loaded, no account or login needed.
