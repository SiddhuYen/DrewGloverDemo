02

Open it

Double-click VCWarmIntro.exe. A console window opens and your browser lands on the app. That console window is the app running, so leave it open while you work and close it when you are done.

Windows will warn you first

You will see “Windows protected your PC” because the file is not signed by a registered publisher. Choose More info, then Run anyway. Nothing is installed and nothing is sent anywhere, the app runs on your own machine at 127.0.0.1.

Before you send the intro

Every hop links to the page that asserts it. Open it. These are real, sourced relationships, but the app has not verified that the two people would vouch for each other, so read the evidence and use your judgement before asking.
